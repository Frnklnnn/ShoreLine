Welcome to ShoreLine

ShoreLine is a respectful web scraper designed to navigate and analyze the vast oceans of information on the internet. It's built with the core principles of responsible web scraping, ensuring compliance with the websites' robots.txt files and employing rate-limiting to minimize impact on web servers.

Our mission is to make data more accessible while maintaining a harmonious relationship with website owners and administrators. ShoreLine is utilized for educational purposes and data analysis.
